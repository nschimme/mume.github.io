# Dragóth Interview

Dragóth the Slayer, of the Lost Legion is a level one hundred Tarkhnarb Soldier.

**Race:** Tarkhnarb Orc
**Class:** Pure warrior

**Special note:**
A long term achievement of a long term player as Dragóth has been around since 1998 and participated in lots of things in between. Among other things, his trophy contains 4 different level 100 characters (including himself) and the record breaking amount of dead Ainur: 19 M's, 9 Mc's, 2 Mb's, 3 Mw's, 1 Ms, 0 Valar and 1 Arata.

### Reference stats:
**Base abilities:** Str:20 Int:9 Wis:8 Dex:12 Con:20 Wil:12 Per:14.
**Most hps ever as an orc (I think?):** 557 max hps.

### Give a brief description of your char (free format telling, class-wise, choice of weapons, style-wise, any other information you wish to include)?
Concussion, two-handed and slashing. Using those weapons I did it all. Open area fights, solohunting, solo vs many, overkilling, trapping, hugefights, follower, leader, you name it.

### How would you summarize the career of your char, how did it all happen?
Some friends showed me a mud at school. Me and player of Vardamir tried another one out, but eventually same friends introduced us to mume and we got stuck.

### What was characteristic to your career, were you especially known of something specific, or did you have some favourite tactics?
At some point I was known to hit really hard. Before concussion got downgraded... Level 70+ were rare back then also, so as a level 70-80 vs normal legends of course I did some damage...

### Did you have somekind of eras distinctive of each other, favourite group mates, etc?
Mostly grouped with p(Vardamir) both as leader and follower during the years. In the beginning I followed Luke alot since it always gave alot of exp.

### What was the one thing you loved in your char that made you play it all the way?
Hard to say, but when I played alot and reached highlevel pretty fast back in 2001-2003 I got stuck at just that, using a "powerchar". I could´ve reached 100 much sooner but some years ago I retired at level 91-93 or so and spent time on/created other characters.

### Describe the best, the worst, and the funniest memory you have with this char?
Hm, first time I got full shining metal was nice. One of the worst...I was nr 1 warlord for the first time in my first shining metal set, and got bashtrapped at moors by NSL using call lightning. It has been plenty of funny moments these years. I have laughed out loud several times.

**Question by Salazár:** Why there are barely any players playing MUME nowadays?
