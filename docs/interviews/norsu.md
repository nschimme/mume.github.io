# Norsu Interview

Norsu has left the building is a level one hundred Tarkhnarb Champion

**Race:** Tarkhnarb Orc
**Class:** Warrior

**Special note:** The first L100 character ever. The only mortal character that is listed on the list of immortals (view wizretired).

### Reference stats:
**Base abilities:** Str:18 Int:10 Wis:7 Dex:14 Con:19 Wil:12 Per:16.
**Reference abilities:** Str:18 Int:10 Wis:8 Dex:15 Con:18 Wil:12 Per:16.

These are probably not what I played with, I rerolled some time after I stopped playing to test something.

### Give a brief description of your char (free format telling, class-wise, choice of weapons, style-wise, any other information you wish to include)?
More than anything else, I depended on having a high track % for PK. Most of the time we'd be a group of 3 people roaming around looking for targets and depend on hunting people down after the initial clash to get kills. Weapon-wise, whatever hit hard I guess. Mostly ornate or warsword. Early Norsu was all warsword I believe, later they did some changes that made ornate more attractive (being able to poison it more times from a vial maybe?)

### How would you summarize the career of your char, how did it all happen?
It happened because a finn (Securitate) rolled an Orc (in a line of many I'm sure), back in the stathunting days of mume, but did not like the stats. I thought they were alright, so I asked if I could have it, and he gave me Norsu...

### What was characteristic to your career, were you especially known of something specific, or did you have some favourite tactics?
*Norsu the Orc* crushes your head extremely hard.
Your body turns numb as the poison speeds to your brain!

### Did you have somekind of eras distinctive of each other, favourite group mates, etc?
With Norsu, I think the people I grouped most with were Caress, Everhate, Uldrak and Baresark. There was a bunch of others too though. We had the world's largest labgroup.

### What was the one thing you loved in your char that made you play it all the way?
The combination I guess?
Orc - more pk targets!
Good tracker - Can't really pk without it.
Melee - We all know what the need for big weapons is a substitute for.

### Describe the best, the worst, and the funniest memory you have with this char?
**The best:** I can't single out any best memory. It was good times whenever there were players to kill, and I did xp whenever there were no pk to be had. I didn't mind running around killing mobs for a few hours at slow times... As long as you ignored Caress's whines of "omg we're gonna kill another troll mob??".
**The worst:** No regrets, it's all good.
**The funniest:** No idea. Highway robbery maybe? The log is out there somewhere. If that was even Norsu? Was one of my orc melee'ers atleast.

### Final thoughts

**When was the primary playing period irl of this char?**
98-04

**What is your relationship with MUME nowadays?**
A fond memory.

**How do you see MUME now compared to what it was back then?**
I don't know how it is for players these days. I just log on and read the news every now and then. Mume was hands down the best player vs player game I've ever played. I don't think you can get that as a new player now. Too small player base probably. It's funny that the management of mume wanted (my impressions) a deep involving game of Tolkiens world with role-playing and such, while frowning on pk as if it was a kind of neccessary evil. And at the same time they created an awesome player vs player game, while for the roleplayers, there's almost nothing that encourages it ingame.
