# Woland Interview

Woland the Professor of Black Magic is a level one hundred Black Númenórean Wanderer

**Race:** Black Númenórean
**Class:** Mage

**Special note:** the first player to have two level 100 characters!

### Give a brief description of your char
Woland is the devil from "Master and Margarita". In the beginning Woland was planned out to be a portalling BN, even though i rarely used the spell.

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
It happened occasionally. After hitting the wall with Azazello I didn't really plan to level Woland to 100. Woland has almost 15M over the top now.

### What was characteristic to your career?
The most characteristic is probably my love for free-riding - i just walk around looking for fight. I hate sitting on my ass somewhere without doing anything.

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
It was almost my only mage char and i always played him for a change after long warrior sessions.

### Questions from other Club 100 members

**Question by Woland:** When was the primary playing period irl of this char?
2001-2005 i think.

### Final thoughts
I don't think anyone needs my hints in the MUME community. Though i cannot pass the possibility to greet all my group-mates and honorable foes!

*Interview done in October 2010 by Antti.*
