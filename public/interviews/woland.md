### Give a brief description of your char
Woland is the devil from "Master and Margarita" and in the book he "used" one "spell" - namely portal. :) So that was it - in the beginning Woland was a planned out to be a portalling BN, even though i rarely used the spell. ;-) Something like a description follows in the "eras" section.

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
It happened occasionally. After hitting the wall with Azazello I didn't really plan to level Woland to 100. This is probably also why I keep playing him when i have time and will - levels are not that important anymore. And we are still getting XP! Woland has almost 15M over the top now (One needs 158M to get to level 100). If I would pk-rip now, I would lose like ~1.8M xp, but since I am already far behind the gap - who cares?

### What was characteristic to your career?
Yeah. Quite some eras. :)
- **Levels 0-25** - pure telnet leveling up.
- **Levels 25-35** - quite some solo hunting or in occasional groups.
- **Levels 35-40/45 (?)** - a lot of grouping with other Macros and some other BN's. I think on telnet also.
- **Levels 45-60/65 (?)** - A lot of grouping with Stolb, Zmej. This is where i realized, that being a BN in a group might be also intersting.
- **Levels 60+** - quake spell got revamped!
- **Levels 79+** - pracced sanc.
- **Levels 80+** - a lot of playing together with Koroviev (2 bns) or Stolb/Zmej.

The most characteristic is probably my love for free-riding - i just walk around looking for fight. I hate sitting on my ass somewhere without doing anything. This naturally means i do not like "trapping" as in "go somewhere, wait till tracks disappear, wait, wait, wait etc".

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
It was almost my only mage char and i always played him for a change after long warrior sessions.

### Describe the best, the worst, and the funniest memory
The best and the worst are hard to pick. I remember many interesting and close fights, but it's really hard to pick one. The most deep feelings are connected to the early ones, but right now i see so many mistakes and bad decisions in them :-) On the other hand the "pro"-fights were mainly calm-headed and do not "ring the bell". Probably the deal is that there were so many of them.

The funniest ... i always loved to make fun of the trappers using watch room :-) I even posted some of them, i think.
