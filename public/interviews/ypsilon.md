# Ypsilon Interview

Ypsilon the Still Wind of the Underdark is a level one hundred Noldorin Quickblade

**Race:** Elf, Noldo
**Class:** Thief

**Special note:** The first white-side character to reach level 100!

### Give a brief description of your char
Back then BRD was the main weapon and Sting on happy days.

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
Hard grinding and crying blood on mobdeaths and more grinding thereafter :D

### What was characteristic to your career?
Obviously it wasn't a well-known fact, but I used to steal a lot from careless players and Rivendell jeweller as long as it worked out... call it thief RP ;)

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
Sneaking, backstabbing... Also the lvl 100 race in the end ... which I won on the white side!
