# Stolb Interview

Stolb The Master Executor of Damage Inc is a level one hundred Tarkhnarb Weaponsmaster

**Race:** Tarkhnarb Orc
**Class:** Warrior

**Special note:** As of now, Stolb is the character with most xp in the game!

### Give a brief description of your char
Stolb is a brutal warrior with great offense, alot of pracs and hp becaouse of levels. I switch weapons along with the pk situation changes around me.

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
Stolb is my 2nd character. I started playing mume as a darkie and didn't have a white character for a year or more. As far as i know, pukes suck, dark side is much more fun.

### What was characteristic to your career?
I always joked that my tactics was just having 3 pedals - hit target, bash target, flee. Sometimes I got confused even between those 3.

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
I guess it would be the ability to survive alot (Usually with Vip's sanc) and deal out tons of dammage.
