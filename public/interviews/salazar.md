# Salazár Interview

Salazár the Forester is a level one hundred Dúnadan Hero

**Race:** Dúnadan
**Class:** Ranger

**Special note:** As of now, Salazár is the character with the shortest MUME playing time to reach level 100: 2 months!

### Give a brief description of your char
A high hp, semihigh defence, pounding mage.

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
I was demoted unfairly by Námo and didn't feel like playing my old chars. Made Salazár as somewhat a copy of Zack but as a man.

### What was characteristic to your career?
I mostly played in a group of three. I like highlevel puke clerics as they are so terribly overpowered :)

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
The ability to lead and tank very well while still being able to nuke and quake things.

### Questions from other Club 100 members

**Question by Salazár:** Why's there barely any players playing now?
Because too many pukes were influenced by Elestir. And also the lack of movement up there in the management.
