# Staub Interview

Staub Steelblade, Chieftain of Durin's House is a level one hundred Dwarven Veteran

**Race:** Dwarf
**Class:** Defense warrior

**Special note:** Balrog 1 - Staub 27. Balrog accounts for Staub's only mobrip.

### Give a brief description of your char
The ultimate defence warrior. Being a dwarf only axes were used for the first 100 levels. 187 def, 94% armour, 520 hps.

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
Decided to try out a defensive warrior, someone that wouldn't die at sight of the first orc. The character turned out to be a bigger success than i ever imagined.

### What was characteristic to your career?
I would always strive for the highest defense and best equipment. Also, xp was always the priority for Staub. Staub never cared about pk.

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
I loved the power to buff anything and to smob with the smallest possible group.
