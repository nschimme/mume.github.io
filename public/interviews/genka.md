# Genka Interview

Genka Beardless, Party Animal of Durin's House is a level one hundred Dwarven Hero

**Race:** Dwarf
**Class:** Warrior

**Special note:** As of now, the youngest / newest level 100 character in the game, created March 2009.

### Your first visit at level 100 lasted less than 12h rl!
After reaching level 100 I logged in the next day and the first feeling was: „What now?“ I was somewhat bored thus... what happened just happened!

### Give a brief description of your char
I used mostly 2 weapon classes, axes or ornate. I used 90% OB ring. Definitely, defence is not my key!

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
I never planned to have a level 100 character. After my whole account was demoted I had a break from MUME. Then one day, I made a decision to create a pure Balrog warrior.

### What was characteristic to your career?
I just exped, learned, motivated myself, found fun!

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
Its OB and HP was all I needed! I loved the contradiction how the enemy characters were afraid of my OB/HP and from another point of view not being afraid of me as a RL person due to my low pk skills…

### Questions from other Club 100 members

**Question by Genka:** What will you do with your account’s password after reaching 100?
I’ll give my password to a friend I trust to have spirit to play and learn MUME.

*Interview done in July 2013 by Antti.*
