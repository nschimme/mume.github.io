# Vardamir Interview

Vardamir Nólimon, the Uncrowned King is a level one hundred Black Númenórean Scholar

**Race:** Black Númenórean
**Class:** Mage/Cleric combo

**Special note:** Was bashed to 1 hps by mobs while collecting the very final xp for level 100!

### Give a brief description of your char
I used to wear heavy armour, a twisted crown, hold a reinforced oak staff and wield an engraved broadsword. Vardamir was always kept at a very young age.

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
It started after I had officially announced that I was leaving the game. I was tired of my other characters and wanted to try out a completely incognito and optimized character.

### What was characteristic to your career?
I was almost always grouped with two Tarkhnarb warriors and I suppose the character was a typical roadkiller.

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
For me it turned out that Vardamir was not as fragile as I thought he would be. I really felt how (insanely) much the character improved in power and ability to survive.
