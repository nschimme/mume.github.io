# Dragóth Interview

Dragóth the Slayer, of the Lost Legion is a level one hundred Tarkhnarb Soldier.

**Race:** Tarkhnarb Orc
**Class:** Pure warrior

**Special note:** His trophy contains 4 different level 100 characters and record breaking amount of dead Ainur.

### Give a brief description of your char
Concussion, two-handed and slashing. Using those weapons I did it all.

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
Some friends showed me a mud at school. Me and player of Vardamir tried another one out, but eventually same friends introduced us to mume and we got stuck.

### What was characteristic to your career?
At some point I was known to hit really hard. Before concussion got downgraded...

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
Using a "powerchar". I could´ve reached 100 much sooner but some years ago I retired at level 91-93 or so.
