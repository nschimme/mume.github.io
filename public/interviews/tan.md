# Tan Interview

Tan N'Ed is a level one hundred Hill Troll Warrior

**Race:** Hill Troll
**Special note:** a level 100 *Troll*, no further comments needed!

### Recalling the sun
I recall that I was slept only 3-5 times total. Only one of them lasted until sunrise though. I think it is part of the game. I would have been angry at myself, not to the other player, if I had sundied.

### Give a brief description of your char
Tan is an unarmed tracking troll with stabbing (spear) as secondary weapon skill. No rescue at all, because mostly played solo.

### How would you summarize the career of your char?
I had not really played troll before, but I noticed that it is a convenient race for such solo player as I had become.

### What was characteristic to your career?
Certainly I was best known for playing solo and doing Warrens/Trollshaws XP. But I regularly went to other places to camp.

### What was the one thing you loved in your char?
I noticed that it is possible to adapt to troll life and have more efficient playing time instead of just idling.

### Questions from other Club 100 members

**Question by Tan:** Which would have been your least favourite race/class to play with similar playstyle?
Probably bear, because AFAIK in bear form they are more limited to certain area.

*Interview done in November 2012 by Antti.*
