# Timodeus Interview

Timodeus the friendly Hobbit-Dragon is a Vala (Architect)

**Race:** Ainu
**Rank:** Vala

### How and when did you find MUME?
Back in 2003 I studied Computer Science. One of my friends was playing MUDs and one of those was MUME, which he showed to us. We used to play from that living room with like 3 to 5 people.

### What keeps you hooked nowadays?
MUME is a very awesome and unique project. Building Tolkien's Arda is a very nice thing. Death matters, danger creates emotion. It might sound masochistic, but that's something no other game delivers in this way.

### From player to Vala
I made like three attempts to level an Istar, all failed somehow. Timodeus is my third or so attempt. I was hanging around as M and Mc and did all sorts of stuff. Then I got a chance to co-build an area.

### Speaking of Ainur, what do they actually do?
They can build own zones, supervise areas and builders, review new areas, fix stuff that is broken, do day-to-day administration. Mudllers write new mudlle.

### Describe the best, the worst, and the funniest memory
The best surely is when a project is done and we open it. It's literally 100s of work hours done by several people over months and years.

The worst part surely is doing justice. Knowing that you just scrap hours, sometimes weeks or months of lifetime a player... it sucks.

The funniest surely was playtesting Angmar. We just busted in on the final boss without much caution and got our asses somewhat nastily kicked.

### Any hints, advices, greetings?
Have fun playing the game, work on it if you wish.

*Interview done in November 2011 by Antti.*
